mu0.ai is an experiment in minimalist AI research insights.

Inspired by the Zen concept of Mu (無) — meaning negation or the emptiness of fixed ideas — this site explores the practice of letting go of preconceived notions in pursuit of truth.

The name draws from the ancient koan and Rich Sutton’s “bitter lesson”: that general methods leveraging computation consistently outperform human-engineered solutions built on assumptions and biases.

Here, less is more. We present only the essential.
